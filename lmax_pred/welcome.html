Computational Analyses of Light Interacting Genes

1. Phylogenetically Informed Annotation (PIA) of the Light Interaction Tool Kit (LIT)

PIA (Phylogenetically-Informed Annotation) finds Light Interacting Genes in transcriptomes or new genomes by efficiently integrating uncharacterized genes into pre-calculated phylogenies of gene families. For more details, refer to the published article. TOOL CURRENTLY UNDER RE-CONSTRUCTION

2. Opsin Phenotyping Tool for Inferring Color Sensitivity (OPTICS)

OPTICS employs machine learning models to accurately predict the lambda-max phenotype of opsins from gene sequences alone. For more details on the database and model training procedures, see the related preprint.
